Fun Facts

  • Under the current food demand, the space of 1.5 earths will be required to sustain the current population. When the population reaches ten billion, three earths space would be required. (World Population History Organization)
  • The Food and Agricultural Organization claims that one third of food produced is wasted through the food supply chain.
  • Kyle Davis states in a research paper of his that anywhere from 1.8-3.1 billion additional people can be fed from a change in dieting (on a global scale.)
  • GMOs contribute a 22% increase in crop yield (Nina Fedoroff.)
  • In 2009, WRAP (Worldwide Responsible Accredited Production) stated that households in the UK throw away 31% of the food they buy
