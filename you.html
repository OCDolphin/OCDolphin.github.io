What can you do to help?

To help in the stuggle against food demand, here are
some things that you can do to help out are:

  • Start eating less, the average human only needs 2000 calories per day,
    most people consume more than this. Make sure you dont eat too little though! As that would be unhealthy.
    Eating a diet without red meat is also very helpful for the solution! This will help the environment
    because there will be less cows creating less methane.
  • You should encourage farms to use GMOS so
    that their crop yeild is higher. You can also boycott businesses that dont use GMO's.
  • Encourage food distributors to use better preservatives when transporting
    their food and to use greater care when handling it.
  • We should invest in an abundant amount of different
    crop types, as some will provide for us now, but may not survive in the
    climate of the future.
